# Sales Development Rep — Role Configuration

## Display Name

Sales Development Rep

## Slug

`sdr`

## Description

The SDR role covers everything a new Sales Development Rep needs to ramp: what the role is, how outreach works across phone, email, and social, how to qualify and book appointments for Account Executives, compensation, performance expectations, and daily operating rhythm. Content is written for people who may have zero B2B sales experience.
