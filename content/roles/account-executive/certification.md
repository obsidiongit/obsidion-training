# Onboarding Certification & Readiness Gate

---

## The Gate

Nobody touches a live prospect until they've passed this gate.

That's not a threat — it's a standard. The Obsidion offer is positioned as a premium, expertise-driven service. The rep sitting across from (or on a call with) a prospect is the proof point before any contract is signed. If you can't clearly explain the product, handle the first three objections, and run a competent discovery call, you're not ready — and sending you into the field before you're ready hurts you, the prospect, and the company.

This certification exists to make "ready" objective rather than a gut feeling.

---

## The Seven Gates

Complete all seven to earn your certification. Every gate must be passed — there are no skips and no substitutions.

### Gate 1 — Complete All Onboarding Sections

Work through every section in the hub. This means actually reading them, not clicking through.

- About the Company
- Product Knowledge (all products)
- Getting Paid & Bonuses
- Tech We Use
- Performance Expectations
- On the Call (overview, scripts, objection handling, call frameworks)
- Daily Structure
- Meeting Cadence
- Accountability

**How to complete:** Mark each section as visited in the hub. No quiz required here — the assessment gates below confirm retention.

### Gate 2 — Pass the Product Knowledge Assessment

Each vertical playbook ends with a scored quiz. You need to pass the quiz for every playbook you'll actively be selling.

- **Passing threshold:** 9 out of 12 correct
- **Re-attempts:** Unlimited — review the relevant sections and retake when ready
- **Verticals to complete:** Cannabis, Food & Hospitality, Salon & Med Spa, Automotive (Contractors quiz coming soon)

These quizzes cover pricing, setup fees, product capabilities, competitive positioning, and outreach strategy. If you can't pass them consistently, you're not ready to answer prospect questions on a live call.

### Gate 3 — Pass the Objection Handling Assessment

A scenario-based assessment covering the most common objections AEs face in the field. You'll be given a prospect statement and must identify the correct response — not just a reasonable one, the right one.

**Objections covered include:**
- "We already have a website"
- "We're happy with what we have"
- "I need to think about it"
- "Send me some information"
- "We don't have the budget right now"
- "We're locked into a contract"
- "Can you guarantee results?"

**Passing threshold:** 75% or better
**Re-attempts:** Unlimited
**Status:** Assessment launching with the certification section. Link will appear here when live.

### Gate 4 — Mock Discovery Call

A live or recorded role-play in which you run a full discovery call with your manager playing the prospect. The call is scored against a rubric.

**What you'll be evaluated on:**
- Opening — did you establish credibility and set the agenda clearly?
- Qualification — did you uncover budget, timeline, and decision-making authority?
- Discovery — did you ask the right questions and listen more than you talked?
- Positioning — did you connect their specific pain to the right Obsidion product?
- Next step — did you close the call with a clear, committed action?

**Passing threshold:** Score 70% or above on the rubric
**Re-attempts:** Anytime. Your manager will schedule a follow-up session.
**How to schedule:** Message your manager on Discord to request a mock call session.

### Gate 5 — Mock Closing Call

A live or recorded role-play in which you present a proposal and close — handling objections as they come up. Your manager plays the prospect.

**What you'll be evaluated on:**
- Proposal presentation — clear, confident, matched to what the prospect said they needed
- Price framing — did you anchor on value before revealing cost?
- Objection handling — did you respond with the right talk tracks or go off-script?
- Trial close — did you ask for the commitment directly?
- Follow-up plan — if they didn't close, do you have a concrete next step?

**Passing threshold:** Score 70% or above on the rubric
**Re-attempts:** Anytime. Your manager will schedule a follow-up session.
**How to schedule:** Message your manager on Discord to request a mock call session.

### Gate 6 — CRM Proficiency Demo

You'll build a sample deal in the CRM from scratch and walk it through the full pipeline. This isn't a test of typing speed — it's a test of whether you understand the pipeline stages and can keep a record accurate.

**What you'll demonstrate:**
- Create a new contact and company record
- Log a discovery call with notes and outcome
- Set the deal stage and close probability correctly
- Add a next step with a due date
- Move the deal through at least two stage changes
- Show what a properly closed-won record looks like

**How to complete:** Record a short Loom or do it live with your manager. They'll confirm completion.

### Gate 7 — Practice Proposal Submission

Submit a completed proposal using the actual Obsidion proposal template — written for a fictional prospect you create. It should be specific enough to feel real: a business name, vertical, specific pain points, recommended products, and pricing.

**What makes a passing proposal:**
- Uses the correct template (no free-form documents)
- Pricing is accurate and tier-appropriate
- Recommended products are justified by the prospect's stated needs
- Language is professional, specific, and free of filler
- Includes a clear next step

**How to submit:** Upload to the shared drive folder or send directly to your manager for review.

## Re-Attempt Policy

There is no penalty for re-attempting a gate. Review the material, practice the rep, and go again. The only rule is that mock calls (Gates 4 and 5) need to be scheduled with your manager — message them on Discord and they'll set a time.

The point isn't to fail people. The point is to make sure the standard is real.

---

## What Happens When You Pass

You're certified. You go live.

Your manager signs off, your certification status is updated, and you move from onboarding to full production. You'll still have your ramp period protections, but you're now an active rep representing Obsidion on real calls with real prospects.

If you're not certified by day 14 of your start date, that's a conversation. Not because the timeline is arbitrary — because the gate covers the basics, and 14 days is enough time to know them.
