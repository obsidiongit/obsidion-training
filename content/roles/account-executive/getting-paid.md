## Overview

Compensation is designed to reward qualified revenue and clean pipeline hygiene. The structure below is representative; your compensation details will be confirmed during onboarding and in your written offer.

## Commission Tiers (Illustrative)

Tiers typically track rolling qualified revenue or gross margin — your plan document defines the meter. Percentages below are placeholders for training purposes.

## How Payouts Are Calculated

1. **Deal marked closed-won in CRM** — The opportunity must be in the correct stage with executed agreement. Verbal yeses do not count until paperwork matches.
2. **Revenue recognition rules apply** — Setup fees may pay on invoice; recurring may pay on first payment received or on schedule. Multi-location rollouts may split across milestones.
3. **Clawback window** — If a client churns or disputes within the clawback period, associated variable comp may be adjusted. Know the window length for your product mix.
4. **Bonus tiers & spiffs** — Quarterly accelerators and team goals stack on top of core commission when published. Spiffs are announced in Slack with explicit dates and caps.

## Important Note

The percentages, thresholds, and dates above are training examples only. Specific numbers, draw terms, and eligibility will always be confirmed by management and your offer documentation.
