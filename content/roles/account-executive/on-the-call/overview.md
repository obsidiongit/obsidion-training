## Call Flow Framework (Quick Reference)

1. **Intro & permission** — Confirm time, name the purpose, ask if now still works. If not, reschedule immediately.
2. **Discovery before pitch** — Map current site, SEO, follow-up, and referrals. Quantify pain where possible.
3. **Tailored pitch** — Tie each product area to a discovery finding. One story beats five feature bullets. Pause for questions every 90 seconds.
4. **Handle objections live** — Label, validate, then reframe. Sound human, not scripted.
5. **Close or next step** — Propose a specific next step. Never end on "I'll follow up sometime."
