# Training Playbooks — Account Executive

## Status: Active — playbooks are the most complete content area

---

## Overview

Vertical-specific sales training. Each playbook covers the market opportunity, product selling angles, pricing, outreach strategy, objection handling, and a quick reference cheat sheet for a specific industry vertical.

After the hub expansion, playbooks focus purely on vertical-specific sales content. Shared topics (company info, tech, compensation, performance, call execution, assets) are covered in the top-level sections.

## Available Playbooks

### Cannabis

Sales guide for the cannabis vertical. Covers dispensary operations, POS integrations, compliance-conscious design, and the unique dynamics of selling digital services to plant-touching businesses.

**Modules**: Welcome, Market Opportunity, Website, SEO, Remarketing, Mobile App, Pricing, Outreach, Objections, Quick Reference, Quiz

### Automotive

Sales guide for auto repair, detailing, body/collision, and tire shops. Covers shop management integrations, local search dynamics, and pricing for automotive verticals.

**Modules**: Welcome, Market Opportunity, Website, SEO, Remarketing, Mobile App, Pricing, Outreach, Objections, Quick Reference, Quiz

### Salon & Med Spa

Sales guide for salons, day spas, and med spas. Covers booking integrations, local SEO for beauty/wellness, retention strategies, and industry-specific selling angles.

### Food & Hospitality

## Post-Extraction Module Structure

After shared modules are extracted to top-level sections, each playbook will contain only these vertical-specific modules:

1. Welcome (vertical intro + market context)
2. Market Opportunity
3. Website (selling angle for this vertical)
4. SEO (selling angle for this vertical)
5. Remarketing (selling angle for this vertical)
6. Mobile App (selling angle for this vertical)
7. Pricing (vertical-specific tiers)
8. Outreach Strategy (vertical-specific messaging)
9. Objection Handling (vertical-specific objections)
10. Quick Reference (cheat sheet)
11. Quiz (assessment)
