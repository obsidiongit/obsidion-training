## What This Section Covers

Obsidion's product line in a role-agnostic way — what each product is, how it works, and why it matters. The vertical-specific *selling angle* for each product lives in the playbooks (e.g., "SEO for Cannabis" is in the cannabis playbook); this section is about understanding the products themselves.

## Products

### [Custom Websites](websites.md)

The foundation of every client engagement. Custom-built sites optimized for performance, conversion, and the client's specific industry — not templates.

### [SEO Services](seo.md)

Local SEO, Google Business Profile optimization, on-page and technical SEO. The system that makes the website findable.

### [Remarketing](remarketing.md)

Automated follow-up campaigns that re-engage visitors who didn't convert on the first touch. Email, SMS, and ad retargeting.

### [Mobile App](mobile-app.md)

Branded mobile applications for businesses that need a direct channel to their customers — loyalty, ordering, booking, and push notifications.
