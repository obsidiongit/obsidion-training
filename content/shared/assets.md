## Headshot Guidelines

A professional headshot is required for LinkedIn, the internal directory, and any co-branded materials. You don't need a photographer.

### Requirements
- **Backdrop**: Clean, neutral background — white, light grey, or dark charcoal. No cluttered environments.
- **Shot quality**: Modern smartphone on a tripod at eye level, in good natural light. No on-camera flash.
- **Framing**: Head-and-shoulders. Face fills 60–70% of frame, centered. Eyes at the upper third.
- **Dress code**: Business casual minimum. Solid colors photograph better than patterns.
- **Delivery**: JPEG or PNG, minimum 1000px on shortest side. Filename: `FirstName-LastName-headshot.jpg`. Send to your manager before publishing.

### Quick self-shoot checklist
1. Stand 3–4 feet from a window for soft, even light
2. Put your phone on a surface at eye level — don't hold it up
3. Shoot 10–15 frames and pick the best one
4. Smile with your eyes — not just your mouth
5. Send to your manager before using publicly
