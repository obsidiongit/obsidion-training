Product 3:
Remarketing
Since cannabis businesses can't run cold ad campaigns, the highest-ROI marketing they can do is re-engaging customers who already exist. That's exactly what we automate.
📲
SMS Campaigns
Text message marketing to opted-in customer lists. SMS open rates exceed 90% — compared to 20–25% for email. Daily deals, new product drops, flash sales, and event invites sent automatically. This is the highest-ROI channel available to dispensaries.
📧
Email Sequences
Automated drip campaigns that run on their own. New customer welcome sequences, win-back campaigns for lapsed visitors, weekly newsletters with promotions — all built once and running forever.
🎁
Loyalty Programs
Points-based rewards, visit streaks, and tier systems that make customers choose this dispensary over the one across the street. Integrated directly into the website and triggered automatically after purchases.
🔄
Win-Back Automation
When a customer goes 30+ days without a visit, the system automatically fires a re-engagement sequence — a personalized SMS or email with an incentive to return. Dead leads become active buyers again without any manual effort.
⚡
Instant Lead Response
Every new form submission, inquiry, or missed call triggers an immediate automated response — within minutes, not hours. Speed to lead is critical; the first business to respond wins the customer the majority of the time.
📊
Campaign Analytics
Real-time dashboard showing open rates, click rates, conversion events, and revenue attributed to each campaign. Know exactly which messages are driving customers back through the door.
Why remarketing is the most important product we sell
1
Acquisition is restricted — retention isn't
You can't run paid ads to find new customers, but there are zero restrictions on communicating with people who've already opted in to your list. That makes the existing customer database the single most valuable marketing asset a dispensary owns — and most of them aren't using it at all.
2
Cannabis is a high-frequency repeat purchase
Unlike buying a car or a mattress, cannabis customers come back weekly — sometimes multiple times per week. Even a small improvement in visit frequency (say, one extra visit per month per customer) compounds across hundreds of regulars into tens of thousands in additional monthly revenue.
3
Most dispensaries have a dead database
The average dispensary has hundreds to thousands of customer contacts sitting completely idle — no follow-ups, no automations, no loyalty logic. We turn that dormant list into an active, always-on revenue engine from day one. It's like finding money they'd already paid for.
4
Speed to response wins the sale
Studies consistently show that responding to a lead within 5 minutes increases conversion rates by up to 900% compared to waiting 30 minutes. Our system responds within seconds — automatically — every time, 24/7. No human needed.
This Is the Stickiest Part of the Retainer
Once a dispensary sees their automated SMS campaign fire off and drive 40 customers back through the door in a weekend — they will never cancel. This is the product that locks in the long-term relationship. Lead every conversation here after you establish trust on the website side.

Module 08 — Sales
Outreach Strategy
You have two major channels: your warm network and Instagram cold outreach. Use them differently.
🤝
Warm Network (Priority)
You know these people. You've worked with them, distributed to them, or seen them at events. Lead with the relationship, not the pitch. Ask about their current situation. Let the conversation get there naturally.
📱
Instagram Cold Outreach
Most cannabis owners live on Instagram. DMs work — but only if they're personalized. Never send a copy-paste pitch. Reference something specific about their brand, then open the door.
Warm Network Approach
1
Start with a genuine check-in
Don't lead with the pitch. Re-engage first. "Hey, how's the [store / brand / season] going?" gives you real intel on their current pain before you say a single word about Obsidion.
2
Find the frustration
Ask about their website, their current marketing setup, how they're handling customer follow-ups. Let them vent. The frustration they describe is your pitch — just solve that specific problem.
3
Introduce Obsidion naturally
"Actually, I've been working with a company that's doing exactly this for dispensaries. It's not what you'd expect — these guys are legit." Then let them ask questions.
Instagram DM Template (cold)
"Hey [Name] — love what you've been doing with [specific detail about their brand/store]. Quick question: are you happy with how your website is performing, or is that something you've been looking to upgrade? Working with a team that's been doing some great stuff for dispensaries in [state] — just thought of you."
Keep it short. Personalize the first line. Ask a question, don't make a statement. You want a reply, not a read receipt.
